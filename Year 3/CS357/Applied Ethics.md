
## Principlism

Composed of 4 ethical principles:
- **Autonomy -** Respect an individual's right to control and decision-making
- **Beneficence -** Do good
- **Non-maleficence -** Minimise harm
- **Justice -** Uphold fairness and equality: distribute burdens/benefits well

Principlism is essentially an application of [Virtue Ethics](Ethical Frameworks#Virtue Ethics) with the above 4 principles selected as the virtues.
